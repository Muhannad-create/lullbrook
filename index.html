About

A free ambient sound library for focus and sleep. Build your own mix: rain over a fire, wind in the trees, the hum of a café. Each layer has its own volume.

No accounts, no ads. Set a sleep timer and the sound fades out gently. Save a mix as a preset. Everything stays in your browser.
